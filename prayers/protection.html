Prayers for Protection

Prayer for Divine Protection

"The LORD is my rock, my fortress and my deliverer; my God is my rock, in whom I take refuge, my shield and the horn of my salvation, my stronghold."

— Psalm 18:2

Heavenly Father,

I come before You today seeking Your divine protection. In a world filled with uncertainty and danger, I find comfort in knowing that You are my shield and fortress. I ask for Your protective hand to cover me and those I love.

Lord, guard my heart, mind, body, and soul from all harm and evil. Station Your angels around me as I go about my day. Protect me on my journeys, in my home, and in all my endeavors.

Help me to dwell in the shelter of the Most High and to rest in the shadow of the Almighty. When fear threatens to overwhelm me, remind me that You are with me always.

Thank You for being my refuge and strength, an ever-present help in trouble.

In Jesus' name, Amen.

Prayer for Spiritual Protection

"Put on the full armor of God, so that you can take your stand against the devil's schemes."

— Ephesians 6:11

Mighty God,

I come before You recognizing that my battle is not against flesh and blood, but against the spiritual forces of evil. Today, I put on the full armor of God that You have provided for my protection.

I fasten the belt of truth around my waist, standing firm in Your Word. I put on the breastplate of righteousness, guarding my heart with right living. I fit my feet with the readiness that comes from the gospel of peace.

I take up the shield of faith to extinguish the flaming arrows of the evil one. I place the helmet of salvation on my head, protecting my mind with the assurance of Your saving grace. And I take up the sword of the Spirit, which is Your Word.

Lord, strengthen me to stand firm against all spiritual attacks, deception, and temptation. Keep my spirit alert and my faith unwavering.

In Jesus' mighty name, Amen.
