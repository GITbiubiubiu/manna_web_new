Prayers for Strength

Prayer for Inner Strength

"I can do all this through him who gives me strength."

— Philippians 4:13

Mighty God,

In times when my strength fails and my resolve weakens, I turn to You, the source of all power. You have promised that those who hope in You will renew their strength, that they will soar on wings like eagles, run and not grow weary, walk and not be faint.

Lord, I need Your strength today. Fill me with Your power when my weakness overwhelms me. When I feel I cannot go on, remind me that I can do all things through Christ who strengthens me.

Help me to persevere through challenges, to stand firm in faith, and to trust in Your unfailing love. May Your joy be my strength, and Your presence my comfort.

Thank You for being my rock and my fortress, my deliverer in whom I take refuge.

In Jesus' name, Amen.

Prayer for Courage in Difficult Times

"Be strong and courageous. Do not be afraid; do not be discouraged, for the LORD your God will be with you wherever you go."

— Joshua 1:9

Faithful Father,

As I face this season of challenge and uncertainty, I come to You seeking courage. You have not given me a spirit of fear, but of power, love, and a sound mind.

Lord, when the path ahead seems dark and daunting, be my light. When obstacles loom large, help me remember that You are greater. When fear threatens to overwhelm me, anchor me in Your perfect love that casts out all fear.

Grant me the courage to face each day with faith, to speak truth with love, and to stand firm in Your promises. Help me to remember that I am never alone, for You have promised to be with me always.

Thank You for Your presence that goes before me, behind me, and beside me in every situation.

In Jesus' name, Amen.
