Prayers for Healing

Prayer for Physical Healing

"Heal me, LORD, and I will be healed; save me and I will be saved, for you are the one I praise."

— Jeremiah 17:14

Loving Father,

I come before You today in need of Your healing touch. You are the Great Physician, and I believe in Your power to restore health and wholeness. I ask for Your healing grace to flow through my body, mind, and spirit.

Lord, I surrender my pain, illness, and discomfort to You. Where there is weakness, bring strength. Where there is sickness, bring health. Where there is pain, bring comfort and relief.

I trust in Your perfect timing and Your perfect will. Help me to be patient in this season of waiting, and fill me with the peace that surpasses all understanding.

Thank You for Your promise that by Your stripes we are healed. I receive Your healing power today.

In Jesus' name, Amen.

Prayer for Emotional Healing

"He heals the brokenhearted and binds up their wounds."

— Psalm 147:3

Compassionate God,

You see the wounds that others cannot see and You understand the pain that words cannot express. I bring before You my broken heart and wounded spirit, asking for Your gentle touch of healing.

Lord, heal the hurts of my past. Remove the sting of painful memories and replace them with Your peace. Where there is grief, bring comfort. Where there is anxiety, bring calm. Where there is bitterness, bring forgiveness.

Restore my joy and help me to see myself through Your eyes—as Your beloved child, worthy of love and belonging. Fill the empty spaces in my heart with Your presence and love.

Thank You for Your promise to bind up my wounds and make me whole again.

In Jesus' name, Amen.
