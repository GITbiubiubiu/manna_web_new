What Are the 10 Commandments?

The 10 Commandments are a set of Godly instructions that God gave to Moses on Mount Sinai, which appear in Exodus 20 and Deuteronomy 5. The 10 commandments are the building blocks of Judeo-Christian moral instruction. They teach us about loving God and loving others.
